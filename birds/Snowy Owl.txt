Snowy Owl.--A male snowy owl, evidently adult, was shot at Indian Springs by a local trapper--on or about December 1, 1929, and sent to us in the flesh by Miss Gertrude Snyder,--then a resident of that place. It was received in good condition and is now number--31263 of the Dickey collection. Other than that the bird was shot near the end of a--week of very cold weather, nothing is known as to the circumstances of the capture.----<img src="http://www.gbbo.org/nbrc/Web_Photos/2014-005%20Snowy%20Owl%20%28Martin%20Meyers%29.jpg" />
